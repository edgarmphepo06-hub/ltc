About Lilongwe Technical College

Lilongwe Technical College provides quality technical and vocational education through practical training, innovation, and industry-focused programs designed to prepare students for employment and entrepreneurship.

Leading Technical Education in Malawi

Lilongwe Technical College stands as a premier institution dedicated to technical excellence and skills development. With state-of-the-art facilities and experienced instructors, we provide hands-on training that prepares students for successful careers.

Our programs are designed in partnership with industry leaders to ensure graduates are job-ready and equipped with the practical skills demanded by today's workforce.

Our Vision

"To be a leading technical and vocational institution producing skilled professionals for national and global development."

Our Mission

"To provide quality, accessible, and practical training through innovation, technology, and industry partnerships."
